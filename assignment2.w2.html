Welcome to Royal Emerald Resort


Royal Emerald River View Resort is located in Nyaung Oo (Bagan) township and is situated right on the Ayeyarwady River (The Million years old and longest River in Myanmar). We offer you all river view rooms, Infinity river view Swimming Pool. It is located just 10 minutes to Shwezigon pagoda and Nyaung Oo Airport.
